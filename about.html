Hi, I'm Jake Caccamo

Introduction

Ok, true, that^ is not me, but I liked the picture. My name is Jake Caccamo and I graduated from Tufts University in May of 2017. I received a BS in Engineering Psychology through the School of Arts and Sciences with Latin Honors of Summa Cum Laude. I currently live in Washington DC and have started my career with Booz Allen Hamilton (McLean, VA) as a UI/UX Designer.

Background

I was born in Mountain View, California. Growing up in the Silicon Valley has given me unique exposure to the tech industry. From early childhood, I played soccer, both as part of a club team and for my middle and high schools. I have maintained close connections to my best friends from the Bay, and they inspire me to work hard and appreciate my roots.

Career Inspiration

As a kid, my "ideal" career changed many times. Before I found my current passion, I was interest in pursuing a career as a psychiatrist. My OCD and anxiety sparked my interest in this field and motivated my desire to learn about how other people think and understand the world. I discovered Engineering Psychology in my second semester at Tufts University. This relatively obscure field of study somehow blended two of my motivating passions: understanding how our brains operate and building advanced technology products. At the intersection of these two ideas, I found my calling as a UI/UX designer. Throughout my time at Tufts University I learned how to optimize usability of technological interfaces by applying principles of user-centered design. I aim to make experiences intuitive for both new and existing users while creating a beautiful overall aesthetic.
